About Us

What this system provides

  • Data entry for products available and sold
  • Calculation of profit made per day, week and month
  • Management of customers and their record

The front end technologies used in this management syatem are HTML, CSS, Bootstrap and for database management the MakeIt team has used (NoSQL) MongoDB with Mongoose.

Other technologies used are node js and express js.
